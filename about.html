About

Hi, I'm Jenny Edison! I am a midwest based graphic designer/artist/student. Though my career is still in its infancy, I am proud of the work I have done in school and in freelance. My main project interests include exploring the neurodiversity of the world, finding beauty in the mundane, and creating products that allow others a more enjoyable and seamless experience. My interests include geography, yoga, trains, dogs, and ice cream.
